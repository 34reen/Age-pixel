AGE PIXEL

Age Pixels is a premier photography company based in the beautiful Ngong region of Kenya,
known for their exceptional outdoor portraits, event coverage, fashion, and travel photography.
Founded by a team of talented and passionate photographers, Age Pixels has become a go-to choice
for clients seeking unique and creative photography services.

Led by a group of experienced professionals, Age Pixels prides itself on its ability to capture
the essence of their clients and their surroundings. Their outdoor portraits showcase the natural
beauty of Kenya's landscapes, while their event coverage captures the excitement and emotion of
special occasions. Their fashion photography is both elegant and innovative, while their travel
photography takes viewers on a breathtaking journey through the sights and sounds of the world.

With a focus on customer satisfaction and attention to detail, Age Pixels has established itself as
a leader in the photography industry. Whether you are in need of a family portrait, wedding coverage,
or a stunning travel photo essay, Age Pixels is dedicated to providing you with exceptional photography
services that exceed your expectations
